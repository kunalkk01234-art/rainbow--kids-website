About Us

Welcome to Rainbow Kids Play School, a nurturing and joyful learning environment dedicated to the holistic development of young minds. Established in 2022, our school provides a safe, caring, and stimulating atmosphere where children can learn, play, and grow with confidence.


We offer Play Group, Nursery, LKG, UKG and Classes 1 to 5. Under the guidance of Principal Mr. Ajay Kishore, we focus on activity-based learning, creativity, confidence building, and academic excellence.
